In China

Introduction

For my summer holiday this year I first went back to China. It`s been two years since my last visit. It was precious time to see my family and friends again. During those two years, my brother got married and had a baby daughter. It was the first time I met my sister-in-law and my niece. My brother got really lucky having them.

After one week at home, I left my hometown Henan province to Beijing, from there I started my tour guide trip. I had worked as a tour guide for four years when I was in my undergraduate, after which I left for my Master`s degree in Europe. I still enjoy doing it when I have the chance. As usual, my group were from Netherlands and Begium. We had a great two weeks trip travelling around China. I made a table to list the highlights we visited.
